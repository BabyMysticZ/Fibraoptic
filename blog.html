In conclusion

Fiber optic internet speed is about twenty times faster than any cable internet connection, and eighty times faster than DSL. With prices ranging between two hundred and four hundred pesos per month, fiber optics is the perfect alternative for most Internet users. But finding fiber optic internet services can be difficult, because its coverage extends to a very small percentage of homes and almost all of them are based in large cities. In addition, the widespread race to spread the use of 5G technology has had an impact on the installation of fiber optic cable connections.
